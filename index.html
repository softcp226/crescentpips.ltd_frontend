What is a Chama, and How Are You Connected to Forex?

A chama is a group of people who come together to save and invest money as a team — it's a trusted and popular way Kenyans build wealth together. At CrescentPips, we’ve taken that same idea and brought it online. We call it a “Chama ya Digital.” Instead of just saving, we use your chama contributions to invest in the global forex market, where money can grow much faster. You don’t need to know how forex works — our expert traders and smart bots handle everything for you.
